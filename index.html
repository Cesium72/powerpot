Empower Your Flowers

Revolutionizing Gardening with Robotics

With PowerPot's unique approach on gardening, you can easily own a beautiful plant in your home without fearing the stress of taking care of it. PowerPot has a large database of both common and endangered plants, and will automatically water your plant as needed. You can buy attachments to warm and illuminate your plant, and PowerPot also can also read the ambient tempurature, humidity, and air pressure of the surrounding environment. You will even get alerts on your phone with an image of you plant if you need to refill the water basin or if your plant needs some help.

Profits Go to Protect Native Species and Biodiverity

As humans leave their deep footsteps on this planet, we bring species to areas where they are not supposed to be. This makes invasive species take over areas, and use up resources, sometimes even killing the native plants. Some frequently seen invasive plants in the U.S. are dandelions and common grass. Native plants are beautiful and help preserve the local ecosystem, so PowerPot donates 30% of profits to help native biodiversity conversation institutes in Denver, like Denver Parks and Rec., and the Denver Botanic Gardens.

For all Plants, Big or Small, Desert or Jungle

Unlike other electronic plant pots, PowerPot comes in many different sizes, so you can take care of plants of any shape and size. You can also customize a water level, water frequency, temperature, and even light amount so that your plant is happy and healthy, always.
